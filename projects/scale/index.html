Scale

Scale is a simple precision measurement software. Available in desktop and phone versions. Here is a demo for a desktop version.

During our CoBot Project, Saleh was going to design arms of our robot in SolidWorks. To do that he needed dimensions of a Servo motor part. He asked me to send him dimensions of the part.

The part was small (barely 1 inch long). I somehow managed to measure the dimensions with the help of LibreCAD(AutoCAD for Linux) but it was not easy.

LibreCAD just gave me pixel positions. I had to do the calculations for length, radius and angle by myself. I searched on the internet for a Photo Measurement software but surprisingly didn't find any software for this purpose. So I decided I will make one myself. A few months before, I tried to mimic a fraction of AutoCAD, and I made MiniCAD with SFML/C++. That time I learned the algorithm for zoom in and zoom out without distorting proportions. I used similar concept and made this Photo Measurement software, Scale.
