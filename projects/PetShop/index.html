PetShop Management

This is PetShop with Pets, Pet Foods, Supplies, Employees, Customers and Admins.

One day I searched on google "Java Projects for Portfolio". It suggested to make A PetShop management app. So I searched again and found this project in Maryam AlBugaey's Github repository. I have to say she has kept the project very well documented. I recreated everything in Spring Boot.

I learned the concepts of role and authority management through this project.
